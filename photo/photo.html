About Me

Hello! I'm Ruchitha Gowda, a passionate photographer based in Bengaluru. I've been capturing moments and telling stories through my lens for 2 years. My photography focuses on capturing the beauty of nature, documenting human emotions, exploring urban landscapes, etc,. I believe that every image has the power to evoke feelings and create lasting memories. I'm always looking for new perspectives and opportunities to express my creativity.
